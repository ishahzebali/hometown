Visit Lahore

Lahore is referred to as cultural heart of Pakistan as it hosts most of the arts, cuisine, festivals, music, film-making, gardening and intelligentsia of the country.

Top 3 activites to do in Lahore

Visit 135 years old market for antique shopping

Lahore, with its rich history and culture, is a great place to buy exotic antique pieces. However, the city has a few antique stores with gems worth taking home.

Take a scenic aerial joyride

Lahore is a city one keeps discovering again and again. What if you discover the city from a bird’s eye view? Yes, you read that right; this is possible in Lahore.

Check out South Asia’s biggest private museum

Lahore is the cultural capital of Pakistan and home to more than a handful of museums with hundreds of historical specimens that represent Pakistan’s rich heritage.
